    EPIC is a desktop productivity monitoring agent for enterprise use.

    Aiprus Software is committed to protecting your privacy while providing productivity monitoring for your organization.

    1 Data Collected

    The Agent collects work-related activity during business hours only:

    Data TypeDescription
    Activity & Usage Apps, websites, keyboard/mouse activity, active/idle hours
    Task & Performance Task completion, productivity score, notifications
    Visual Monitoring Random screenshots & short video clips (per company policy)
    System & Device Device name, IP address, login/logout times, user ID

    ⚠ The Agent does NOT collect:

    • Passwords or authentication credentials
    • Personal files or non-work-related activity
    • Financial transactions or personal financial information

    2 Purpose of Data Use

    • Generate productivity reports and dashboards
    • Support fair, unbiased performance evaluations
    • Enable workload planning and efficiency improvements

    No data is sent outside your organization.

    3 Data Storage & Security

    • Data is stored securely within your organization
    • All transmitted data is encrypted in transit and at rest
    • Role-based access — only authorized personnel can view data
    • Screenshots and video clips captured per company policy

    4 User Consent

    By installing and using the Desktop Agent, you acknowledge and consent to monitoring of your work-related activity during business hours for productivity reporting purposes only.

    5 User Responsibilities

    • Use the Desktop Agent for authorized work purposes only
    • Keep your credentials secure — do not share accounts
    • Follow company policies regarding the Desktop Agent
